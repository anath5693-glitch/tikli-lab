No. You do not need to know the exact mix before you reach out. The initial audit helps clarify what matters most for your goals and current visibility.

Can you work with my existing website and campaigns?

Yes. PinRup AI Lab can work with your existing site and campaigns, or help you rebuild key pieces if that will create a better long‑term foundation.

Timelines depend on your current starting point and mix of services. SEO and authority building take longer, while paid search and Meta Ads can start generating data and opportunities much faster.

No. PinRup AI Lab is based in Ontario and works remotely with businesses across Ontario and Canada.

Can you work with internal teams or other agencies?

Yes. We often plug into existing teams or external partners and focus on the visibility, AI, and search pieces that need the most attention.
