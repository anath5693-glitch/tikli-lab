What does PinRup AI Lab focus on?

PinRup AI Lab focuses on AEO, GEO, SEO, Google Ads, and Meta Ads for businesses that want a more modern digital growth system.

Is GEO the same as SEO?

No. GEO supports discoverability in generative AI environments, while SEO supports traditional organic search.

Can this work for local businesses?

Yes. Local and service-based businesses can benefit from technical SEO, local signals, strong service pages, and answer-ready content.
